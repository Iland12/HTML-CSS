DEFINITION:
A border in CSS is a line tha wraps around an element's box 
(like paragraphs, divs, images, etc.). You can customize its width, 
style, color, and radius.


BORDER PROPERTIES:
|PROPERTIES|			|DESCRIPTION|								|EXAMPLE|
'border'				Shorthan foe width, style, and color		'border: 2px solid red;'
'border-width'			Sets thickness of the border				'border-width: 3px;'
'border-style'			Style: 'solid', 'dashed', 'dotted', etc.	'border-style: dash;'
'border-color'			Sets color of the border					'border-color: red;'
'border-radius'			Round the corners of the border				'border-radius: 1px;'


SHORTHAND SYNTAX:
border: 3px dashed orange;
/* width | style | color */
